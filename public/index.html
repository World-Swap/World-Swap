How your money stays safe

Escrow, without a middleman holding your cash.

The hardest part of paying a stranger online is trust. World Swap solves it with escrow that lives in code — not in our bank account.

1

You pay into escrow

Your USDC locks inside an escrow contract the moment you buy or hire. It's out of your wallet, but it hasn't reached the seller yet.

funds locked
2

They deliver

The seller ships your item or the freelancer does the work. You can see the status the whole time — shipped, in progress, delivered.

delivery tracked
3

You release

Happy? Release the funds and the seller gets paid instantly. Not happy? Open a dispute and the funds stay frozen until it's resolved fairly.

released on your OK
World Swap never holds your money. The contract does — and only you can release it.
